## App Description

```
FuelRx takes the mental load out of eating well. We help CrossFit athletes answer one question each week: "What should I buy and cook to fuel my training?"

BUILT FOR ATHLETES
Meal plans designed for CrossFit athletes who need real nutrition to support intense training.

COMPLETE WEEKLY PLANS
Get a full week of meals with recipes, macro breakdowns, and grocery lists—generated by AI and refined by real athletes.

SMART GROCERY LISTS
Shop efficiently with automatically categorized ingredient lists. No more wandering the aisles.

SNAP A MEAL
Photograph your food for instant nutritional analysis. Build your personal cookbook effortlessly.

COMMUNITY POWERED
Browse meals from athletes like you. Save favorites and swap into your plan with one tap.

CONVENIENCE OVER COMPLIANCE
We're not a tracking app. Generate your plan, shop, prep, eat, repeat. Stop thinking about food and start performing.

Download FuelRx and take the guesswork out of nutrition.
```
